Our Services

Revolutionizing Healthcare with Technology

DoctorSina is reinventing the way we access medical care. Through its state-of-the-art virtual platform, DoctorSina delivers convenient, efficient, high-quality healthcare services for everyone.

Computer-aided Diagnosis

AI-based applications help healthcare professionals diagnose diseases by analyzing patients' symptoms, lab tests, and medical history.

Clinical Decision Support

AI-based apps provide personalized treatment recommendations based on patients' medical history, symptoms, and diagnostic data.

Medical Image Analysis

AI helps professionals interpret X-rays, MRIs, and ultrasounds by providing automated analysis to aid diagnosis.

Patient Monitoring

Monitor patients remotely, collect health data, and get alerts for significant changes to provide timely interventions.

Chronic Disease Management

Manage chronic diseases with AI apps that provide medication reminders, lifestyle advice, and personalized exercise programs.

Home Care Assistance

AI helps patients manage health at home with medication reminders, lifestyle tips, and connecting them to healthcare professionals.
